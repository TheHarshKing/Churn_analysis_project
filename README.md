# Churn_analysis_project
This project focuses on customer churn analysis, using historical customer data to identify patterns and behaviors that indicate a likelihood of leaving the company. Machine learning models (such as Logistic Regression, Random Forest, or XGBoost) are used to predict churn probability, enabling businesses to take timely, data-driven retention action
# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard built using **Power BI** to analyze customer churn patterns, identify revenue loss, and provide actionable business insights for improving customer retention.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses. Losing customers directly impacts revenue and long-term business growth.

This dashboard helps stakeholders understand:

- Customer churn distribution
- Churn by contract type
- Churn by internet service
- Revenue lost due to churn
- Customer tenure analysis
- Overall churn rate

The insights generated from this dashboard help businesses improve customer retention and make data-driven decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

A telecom company is experiencing customer churn, leading to significant revenue loss. The management wants to identify the major factors contributing to churn, understand customer behavior, and develop effective strategies to retain valuable customers.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn patterns.
- Identify high-risk customer segments.
- Measure customer churn rate.
- Analyze churn across different contract types.
- Identify which internet services have higher churn.
- Measure revenue loss due to churn.
- Analyze churn by customer tenure.
- Provide actionable business recommendations.

# 📊 Dashboard KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Total Customers | Total number of customers |
| Total Churn Customers | Customers who left the company |
| Active Customers | Current active customers |
| Churn Rate (%) | Percentage of customers who churned |
| Monthly Revenue Lost | Revenue lost because of churn |

---

# 🔍 Key Insights

- Month-to-Month customers have the highest churn rate.
- Fiber Optic users are more likely to churn than other service users.
- New customers show the highest churn percentage.
- Long-term customers have the lowest churn rate.
- Revenue loss is highest from Month-to-Month customers.
- Contract duration has a strong impact on customer retention.
- Active customers still represent the majority of the customer base.
- Customer segmentation helps identify high-risk groups for retention campaigns.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
- Investigate issues related to Fiber Optic services.
- Launch personalized customer retention campaigns.
- Offer loyalty rewards to long-term customers.
- Monitor churn KPIs regularly.
- Improve customer support quality.
- Use predictive analytics to identify customers likely to churn.
